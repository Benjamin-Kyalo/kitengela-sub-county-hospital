Welcome to Kitengela Sub-County Hospital

Kitengela Sub-County Hospital is a Ministry of Health primary care hospital located in Oloosirkon/Sholinke (Noonkopir Road) Kajiado East in Kajiado County.As of 2024, the facility was fully operational with a capacity of 84 beds. It is regulated by Ministry of Health under registration number 14950.
